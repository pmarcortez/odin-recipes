Chilaquiles Verdes

Description

Chilaquiles Verdes is a traditional Mexican dish made from lightly fried corn tortillas covered in a bright and flavorful salsa verde. Serve it for breakfast with a fried egg, or for lunch and dinner with a side of refried beans.

Ready in 30 minutes from start to finish, this recipe is gluten-free and vegetarian.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tomatillos (husked and rinsed)
  • 3 jalapeno peppers stemmed and seeded
  • 1/2 medium white onion
  • water (as needed)
  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 2 cups garlic
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice (about 1/2 lime)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil (for frying)

To Serve

  • cotija cheese
  • chopped cilantro
  • diced white onions
  • sour cream or Mexican crema
  • fried eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Prep salsa ingredients. In a medium pot, add the tomatillos, jalapeños, onion, and enough water to completely cover them. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 8 minutes.
  2. Blend salsa ingredients. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the tomatillos, jalapeños, and onion to a blender. Add the cilantro, garlic, lime juice, and salt and blend until smooth. Taste and add more salt as needed.
  3. Prep tortillas. Stack the corn tortillas on top of each other and cut them into eighths to create small triangle wedges. Line a large plate with paper towels and set aside.
  4. Preheat oil. Working in batches, heat 2 tablespoons of the vegetable oil in a large sauté pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry tortillas. Add half of the tortillas and fry for 8 minutes, stirring every minute or so, until they are crispy. Transfer them to the lined plate to drain and repeat the frying proess with the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil and tortilla wedges.
  6. Coat tortillas. Add all of the fried tortillas back to the pan and pour the salsa verde on top. Gently toss together to coat.
  7. Assemble and serve. Serve immediately and garnish with cotija cheese, cilantro, onions, and Mexican crema.
